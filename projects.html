My vision for utilizing AI in frontend

When looking at the future of frontend design, I see collaboration with AI powered tools as an integral part of everyday processes. I have no qualms about AI taking over tedious tasks like debugging, 3D-modeling, and div-centering (we all know).
When it comes to digital IP there needs to be clear and consistent guidelines for how (and whether) that IP can be used by AI. Regulations ensure compliance and infringement on these regulations will result in unfavourable results.

Some industry professionals have engaged in discourse about the facts of AI and how it works, semi-effectively damping the general population likening it to Skynet. I believe that fear stems from not understanding the technology, so spreading technology literacy is in my opinion key to be able to utilize AI effectively.
