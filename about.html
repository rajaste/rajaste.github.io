Who am I anyway?

I'm a budding designer from Helsinki, Finland. I study IT in Haaga-Helia, specializing in digital services and UI/UX.

My passion

My passion for design was ignited through the same everyday difficulties people have with suboptimal product design on websites, apps, and the like. Understanding what happens behind a misplaced button, a broken element, or ineffective design principles made me wonder how these problems could be fixed.
A lot of user-centered design to me is understanding that the way that a user navigates through a product IS the blueprint. Designers must meet the user’s expectations of a product, considering what preset ideas we have about using said product. For example, we all understand what a magnifying glass icon on top of a webpage means without needing the label “search”. Following such breadcrumbs, we can craft an efficient, user-friendly product with exquisite design.
